## 📋 What Templates Include

### Rules Template (7 Sections)
```
1. Eligibility - Who can enter
2. Submissions - How to submit (format, size, etc.)
3. Content - What's allowed/not allowed
4. IP Rights - Copyright ownership
5. Disqualifications - Why you'd be disqualified
6. Judging - How winners are selected
7. Prize Claim - How to claim prize
```

### Terms & Conditions (10 Sections)
```
1. Agreement - Legal acceptance
2. Your Responsibilities - What you agree to
3. Intellectual Property - Copyright rights
4. Liability - What we're not responsible for
5. Privacy - How we handle your data
6. Disqualification - When we ban participants
7. Modifications - We can change terms
8. Disputes - How to resolve conflicts
9. Entire Agreement - Legal completeness
10. Contact - How to reach us
```

## 🎯 How to Use

### For New Competition
```
1. Go to Create Competition
2. Scroll to "Rules" section
3. Click "Use Default" button
4. Template populates instantly
5. Customize if needed
6. Do same for Terms & Conditions
7. Save competition
```

### To Reset Rules
```
1. Go to Edit Competition
2. Click "Use Default" again
3. Fresh template loads
4. Or keep existing and customize
```
